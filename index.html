About Tellavista

Tellavista is Africa’s #1 AI-powered education platform built to help students master digital skills, coding, and artificial intelligence in a simple and personalized way. We believe education should not be one-size-fits-all, so we’re using AI to create adaptive learning paths for every student — from beginner to expert.

Our mission is to make world-class education accessible to every African student, regardless of their background, device, or location. With AI tutors, interactive learning modules, and gamified lessons, Tellavista helps students learn faster, stay motivated, and achieve real career growth.

We’re not just building a platform. We’re building a movement — one that empowers the next generation of African innovators to create, solve, and lead using technology.

Tellavista is proudly founded by Abdul Afeez Adewale Tella and driven by a clear vision: to transform how Africans learn and use technology to change the world.
