About Nelavista

Nelavista (formerly Tellavista) helps students understand complex topics through personalized explanations, quizzes, and AI-powered support. Our mission is to make quality education accessible to every student in Africa through smart learning technology and motivation-driven teaching.

About the Founder

Abdul Afeez Adewale Tella is a Nigerian mathematician, animator, and AI innovator on a mission to make education smarter, simpler, and more accessible for millions of students.
