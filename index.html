Welcome to Tellavista

Tellavista is Africa’s #1 AI-powered education platform helping students learn faster, smarter, and in their own style. Built by Abdul Afeez Adewale Tella, Tellavista transforms education with intelligent AI tutors that guide each learner personally.

About Tellavista

Tellavista helps students across Africa access personalized learning experiences powered by artificial intelligence. It bridges the gap between quality education and accessibility, using AI to adapt to every learner’s pace, weakness, and goals — making education more human, affordable, and effective.

About the Founder

Abdul Afeez Adewale Tella is a Nigerian mathematician, animator, and AI innovator on a mission to revolutionize learning across Africa. He founded Tellavista to make education personal, intelligent, and accessible to every student — regardless of background.
